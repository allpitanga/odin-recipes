Filardi's Hot Cocoa

A recipe my friend Gabriel teached me

Ingredients

  • 300ml of milk;
  • Maisena;
  • Nescau;
  • Vanilla essence;
  • Cinnamon powder;
  • Condensed milk.

Steps

  1. Add 1/4 of a mug of milk on a pot;
  2. Dissolve the maisena at the pot by mixing;
  3. Add the rest of the milk;
  4. Add 3 tablespoons of Nescau;
  5. Add 2 teaspoons of Maisena;
  6. Add a little bit of vanilla essence;
  7. Add a little bit of condensed milk;
  8. Add a little bit of cinnamon;
  9. Wait until its boiling (mix every now and then);
  10. Voil√°!
