Lui's Pancakes

Description

The pancake recipe my girlfriend Lui taught me.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg;
  • 100ml of milk;
  • 125g of flour;
  • 3 teaspoons of baking powder;
  • Pinch of salt;
  • Sugar to taste.

Steps

  1. Mix the egg and milk;
  2. Add flour until you get a nice, solid, consistency;
  3. Mix everything until its homogeneous;
  4. Add the salt, but just a pinch (less than half a teaspoon);
  5. Add sugar until it tastes amazing;
  6. Success!
